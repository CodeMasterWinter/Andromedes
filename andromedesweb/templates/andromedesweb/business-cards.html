What is a virtual business card?

Nowadays, All the information we share about ourselves and our business is contained online. Whether it be your Social Media accounts, your digital portfolio or your ACTUAL BUSINESS! And more importantly, when people want to find you, their first instinct is to find you ONLINE, which is where we come in.

A virtual business card grants people access to all the information you want to share ALL IN ONE PLACE. You can share as much information as needed, unlike a physical business card, which is traditionally limited to just your Name, Job, Phone Number and E-mail Address.
